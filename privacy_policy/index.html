TL;DR — The Short Version

Smart Storage Cleaner works entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data or files. Storage permissions are used only to display and delete files you explicitly choose to remove.

📖

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Storage Cleaner ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Android application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users of the Smart Storage Cleaner app available on the Google Play Store.

🔍

2. Information We Collect

✅ We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information, files, or usage data to any external server.

All file scanning, analysis, and deletion happens entirely on your local device. No file names, file contents, or usage statistics are ever sent outside of your phone.

The app may use anonymous crash reporting through Google Play Services (if applicable), which is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

🔑

3. Permissions We Use

The app requires the following Android permissions to function:

📂
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Allows the app to scan and display files stored on your device's internal storage and SD card.
🗑️
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Allows the app to delete files that you explicitly select for removal.
⚙️
MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Required on Android 11+ to access and manage all files for comprehensive storage analysis. This is used only for local scanning — no data leaves your device.
📶
INTERNET
Used solely to display Google AdMob advertisements. Ad content is loaded from Google's servers. No user file data is transmitted.
📢

4. Advertisements

Smart Storage Cleaner uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data such as device identifiers for the purpose of serving personalized or non-personalized ads.

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control or access any data collected by AdMob.

ℹ️ Ad-related data is handled entirely by Google AdMob and is separate from any app functionality.
💾

5. Data Storage & Security

🔒 All app preferences and scan results are stored locally on your device only.

We do not operate any servers or databases that store your data. The app does not require you to create an account or log in.

Since no data is transmitted, there is no risk of your files being exposed through our servers or services.

🔗
